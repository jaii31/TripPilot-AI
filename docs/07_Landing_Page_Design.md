# TripPilot AI - Landing Page Design

## Objective

The landing page should immediately communicate that TripPilot AI is a premium AI product rather than a traditional travel booking website.

The experience should feel intelligent, modern, minimal, and trustworthy while encouraging users to begin planning their trip.

---

# Design Principles

- Premium
- Minimal
- Dark Theme
- Interactive
- Responsive
- Smooth Animations
- Purposeful Motion
- No Clutter
- Explain Before Impress
